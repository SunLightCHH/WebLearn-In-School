One particular book which is recommended
reading is The street Lawyer by John Grisham.
This book is about a lawyer who begin re-
evaluating his ptiorities in life when a bad
incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently,
he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets,
and realize the harsh existence of the homeless,
and vows to give them a chance in courts. The
Street Lawyer is great book. It is well written
and interesting. Other books by John Grisham
include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The
Client.
